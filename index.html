About Me

Global health & policy enthusiast focused on advancing Health Equity, Social Innovation, and Urban Health. Holding a PhD in Global Health, my career spans among others consulting for the World Health Organization, co‑founding the social‑impact partnership Impact Thinkers, and conducting research on the social determinants of health.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an eight‑week, hands‑on programme that teaches how to leverage modern AI tools — without coding — to streamline professional and academic workflows.

  • Automating documents, spreadsheets & data workflows
  • AI‑powered literature reviews & knowledge management
  • Building custom chatbots & retrieval‑augmented systems
  • Creating visuals & presentations with generative models
  • Capstone project to showcase applied skills

Capstone Project

For the course capstone, I created a custom GPT — Health Equity Policy Reviewer — designed to help users critically assess policy documents for the integration of key public‑health approaches. The GPT:

  • Summarizes the uploaded document
  • Counts and evaluates how frequently specified approaches are mentioned
  • Assesses whether those approaches are substantively incorporated
  • Poses follow‑up questions to prompt deeper reflection

It exclusively analyzes the document provided in each session and does not reference external documents unless instructed. Try it here:
